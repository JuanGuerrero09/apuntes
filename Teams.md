### **Strategic Report: How to Use the Team**

#### **1. The "Immunity Pivot" System**

The core strength of this team is the ability to switch between your two restricted Pokémon.

- **If a Fighting move is coming at Terapagos:** Switch to Calyrex-Shadow.
    
- **If a Ghost move is coming at Calyrex:** Switch to Terapagos.
    
    This allows you to preserve your "Restricted" Pokémon's health while forcing the opponent to waste turns.
    

#### **2. The "Physical Terapagos" Secret Tech**

Magicaura discovered a unique interaction with the Stellar Tera form:

> **The Mechanic:** _Terra Starstorm_ calculates damage based on whichever offensive stat is higher (Attack or Special Attack). If the opponent uses _Snarl_ or _Struggle Bug_ to lower Terapagos's Special Attack, and you have a **31 Attack IV**, the move automatically flips to a Physical attack. Since Pokémon like Snarl-users (Incineror, Chi-Yu) usually have lower Physical Defense, you can bypass their debuffs entirely.

#### **3. Speed Control as a Win Condition**

This team does not use Tailwind. Instead, it relies on **Flutter Mane** to cripple the opponent’s speed:

- **Thunder Wave:** For permanent speed drops on key threats like Miraidon or Koraidon.
    
- **Icy Wind:** For spread speed control that breaks Focus Sashes.
    
- **Taunt:** To stop Trick Room or opposing setup.

### **Lead Strategies & Matchups**

#### **Vs. Calyrex-Shadow + Zamazenta (The Mirror/Standard)**

- **Lead:** Flutter Mane + Ogerpon-C
    
- **Back:** Terapagos + Calyrex-S
    
- **Plan:** Use Flutter Mane to slow down their Calyrex. Use Ogerpon’s _Knock Off_ to remove items like Choice Specs or Assault Vests. Once the speed advantage is yours, bring in Terapagos to set up _Calm Mind_.
    

#### **Vs. Koraidon (Sun Teams)**

- **Lead:** Flutter Mane + Calyrex-S
    
- **Back:** Terapagos + Incineroar
    
- **Plan:** Terapagos’s _Teraform Zero_ ability is crucial here—it clears the Sun, weakening Koraidon’s Fire moves and Protosynthesis buffs. Use Calyrex’s _Will-O-Wisp_ to burn Koraidon immediately.
    

#### **Vs. Miraidon (Electric Terrain)**

- **Lead:** Rillaboom + Ogerpon-C
    
- **Back:** Terapagos + Calyrex-S
    
- **Plan:** Use Rillaboom to override Electric Terrain immediately. Ogerpon’s _Follow Me_ can redirect Miraidon’s attacks while you safely pivot into your heavy hitters.

### **Key Execution Tips**

1. **Heal the Shell:** Use Calyrex’s _Pollen Puff_ on Terapagos. If Terapagos is at 100% HP, its "Tera Shell" ability activates, making it almost impossible to one-shot.
    
2. **Rocky Helmet Chip:** Switch Incineroar into Urshifu’s _Surging Strikes_. The three hits will trigger Rocky Helmet three times, putting Urshifu into range for a _Grassy Glide_ or _Stellar Tera Starstorm_ KO.
    
3. **Burn Early:** Between Incineroar and Calyrex, you have two _Will-O-Wisp_ users. Prioritize burning Zamazenta or physical attackers to make Terapagos immortal.
