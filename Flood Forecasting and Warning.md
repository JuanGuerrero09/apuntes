**Flood forecasting, warning and response process**

- Monitoring: **Measure** hydrological and meteorological data
- Forecasting: **prediction** of water levels and discharges
- Warning: **Decide** to **act** and **disseminate** to public and authorities
- Response: Take **action** to **reduce damage** and **lost of life**

**Warnings and Thresholds**

Decision makers only act when a threshold is passed.
