
Project summary*

Tell us about your proposed solution. Summarise in your own words what problem your project is aiming to solve, why is the issue significant, and who will benefit from your solution.

This project aims to develop an open-source toolkit for automatically detects physically inconsistent and hydrologically implausible behaviour in machine-learning-based streamflow forecasts and existing datasets. The validation phase of the machine-learning models is really important since is what determines how reliable is the information obtained by the model.

By providing an easy-to-use, open-source framework, this toolkit will allow modelers to integrate physical validation into their existing workflows. This standardized approach will reduce the time required to verify new models and ensure that AI-driven forecasts are not just mathematically valid, but hydrologically realistic.
